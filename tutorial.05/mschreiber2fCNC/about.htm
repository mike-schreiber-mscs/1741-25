About Schreiber Custom CNC Routing

Schreiber Custom CNC Routing is a small custom cnc routing business out of Buffalo City, WI. We offer state of the art signage, manufacturing, prototyping, guitar bodies, photo carving, and 3D scultping.

Mike Schreiber

Mike Schreiber, owner of Schreiber Custom CNC Routing, started in the sheet metal fabrication industry 22 years ago. It all began with operating CNC equipment in sheet metal fab shops. After 11 years of experience in CNC machine operation, he moved into the engineering side of manufacturing where he programmed CNC machines such as router, waterjet, and turret punch to name a few. Also in the mix was new equipment implementation and manufacturing process improvement practices. In 2007, Mike joined a CAD/CAM Software company where he acquired industry knowledge while supporting customers in a software application engineer role. During this time he also conducted onsite software training to customers. In 2013 he completed the ISTQB (International Software Testing Qualifications Board) Certification Certified Tester Foundation Level. This brings us to 2015 when Mike moved forward on an idea to apply some of that manufacturing knowledge and start up Schreiber Custom CNC Routing. Mike having 20 plus years of CNC experience coupled with the knowledge of the latest in CAD/CAM technology, you can be assured to get the highest of detail, accuracy, and design for your project needs.
