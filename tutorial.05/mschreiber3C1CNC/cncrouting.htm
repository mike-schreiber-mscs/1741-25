What is CNC Routing?

A CNC Router is used to cut, trim, and shape a wide variety of materials including wood, plastic, composites, foam, honeycomb core and non-ferrous metals into either flat or three-dimensional shaped products. CNC routing is recommended when 3D carving or any milling needs to be done, beveled or rounded edge treatments need to be applied, or the material is a non-ferrous metal.
